Graphic Designer

Home Job Info

Career Overview

Description

Graphic Designers create visual concepts using computer software or by hand to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines, and reports.

Average Salary

The national median annual wage for Graphic Designers is approximately $58,910 per year ($28.32 per hour), according to the O*NET / Bureau of Labor Statistics data.

Employers

Graphic Designers are hired across a wide range of industries, including:

  • Specialized Design Services
  • Printing and Related Support Activities
  • Advertising, Public Relations, and Related Services
  • Publishing Industries (except Internet)
  • Computer Systems Design and Related Services
  • Self-Employed / Freelance
