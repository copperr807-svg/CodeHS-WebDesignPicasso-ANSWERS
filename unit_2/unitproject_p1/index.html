What I Do

My high altitude balloon (HAB) project is something that I am proud to have completed. I am currently working on a second balloon but my first one had a camera, battery, and GPS. The second one I'm designing is based off of the previous failures and I programmed / soldered four ICs to collect data and store it on a microSD card.

Another thing I like doing is programming, specifically in C. I enjoy C because it is a very universal language that can be used for formatting, video games, UIs, and more. I am learning assembly on the side too. This is because assembly could be a good learning experience that also teaches me how firmware / hardware works.
